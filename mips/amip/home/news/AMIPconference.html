Proceedings of the First AMIP Scientific Conference

 

The first AMIP Scientific Conference was held 15-19 May 1995 in Monterey, California. The Conference provided a forum for the comprehensive examination of AMIP modeling results to date. A collection of presentation abstracts is available  from the AMIP Project Office (amip@pcmdi.llnl.gov). Abstracts of subsequent AMIP-related work also are continuously archived online.

The Proceedings of the AMIP Scientific Conference have been published as a report of the World Climate Research Programme (WCRP):

Proceedings of the First International AMIP Scientific Conference
(Monterey, California, USA, 15-19 May 1995)
WCRP-92, WMO/TD-No. 732, 532 pp.
