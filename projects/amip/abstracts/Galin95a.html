Galin, V. Y., 1995a: AMIP runs by the DNM GCM. DNM Memorandum, 6 pp. (Available from V. Ya Galin, Department of Numerical Methematics Russian Academy of Sciencs, Leninsky Prospect, 32A, Moscow 117334, Russia.)

In this publication we present some results of numerical experiments which have been carried out with a new version of the DNM GCM. The previous version of this model (see Phillips, 1994) unfortunately contained incorrect code for the calculation of the vertical velocity. The consequences of this error and results of its correction are described here.
